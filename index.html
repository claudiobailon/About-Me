Getting to know Claudio

Hello,
My name is Claudio Bailon-Schubert, thanks for coming to my webpage! I grew up in a small farming town in Eastern Washington and graduated from High School in 2007. I then attended Whitworth University majoring in Math and Computer Science. I only attended one year and eventually made my way to Washington State University to study Civil Engineering. That also only lasted a year before I ran out of money and was unsure about my desired career path.

Since then, I've worked a wide variety of jobs. I spent a lot of time delivering pizza and auto parts, working retail, working at the airport, and, most recently, as a surveyor. Now I'm taking to dive into software development and hoping to find a way to leave my mark on the word.
